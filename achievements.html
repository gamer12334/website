  • To keep the curriculum in line with industry requirements, department has introduced subjects in the curriculum, in collaboration with IT industries like Infosys, Office Box solutions and RGen software solution(I) Pvt. Ltd.
  • Department encourages the students to undertaker industrial training and visits to get adequate exposure of developments in IT field.
  • The department regularly conducts guest lectures of industry experts and academicians to keep the students abreast with the current technologies.
  • The different areas of research undertaken by the department are in the fields of Networking, Network Security, Natural Language Processing, Cloud Computing and Wireless Sensor Networks.
  • MOUs with companies like Infosys, Infocepts, Praxis Logica, Persistant have brought the required support and training on the latest software products which has helped in bridging the gap between institution and industry.
  • RCOEM is the partner college of Infosys for their Campus Connect initiative. IT Department is the center for various activities of Campus Connect.
  • The Department offers elective courses jointly developed by RCOEM and companies like Infosys and Infocepts. The courses include Electives – Business Intelligence by Infocepts, Nagpur, Software Testing and Mobile Application Development by Infosys, Pune.
  • Under RGSTC-TIFAC-MSME Internship Scheme, one team was involved in development of module – “Real Time Logging and Monitoring System for Electromechanical Machines” in association with Khosla Engineering Tools, Nagpur, during May-June 2015.
  • The group of students from the department has been selected for developing solutions to industry problems under RGSTC-TIFAC-MSME scheme.
