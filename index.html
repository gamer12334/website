About Us

About Department

The Information Technology Branch of Shri Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management, popularly known as RCOEM, was established in 2001 with a vision to contribute to the IT world by focusing on education and research, entrepreneurship and innovation. Accredited twice by National Board of Accreditation, AICTE, our Branch is renowned for delivering exceptional Technical and Communication skills.

Since its inception, with its unique model of education, research, and industry interaction, has grown in stature to become a large community of considerable repute in academic as well as corporate circles.

Vision

  • To establish the department as a major source of human resource for the IT sector.

Mission

  • To produce engineering graduates with sound technical knowledge in Information Technology, good communication skills and ability to excel in a professional career.

Latest Courses

Computer Programming.

The Computer Programming courses prepares you for a career in software development. The program also specializes in program development strategies using C, C++, Java, Python, database design and database administration. Learning programming at a young age helps your children solve everyday problems and get set up for a lifetime of opportunities.

Full Stack Development

This course introduces the basic strategies and techniques required to develop a website. The students will be able to design and develop a well-designed portable website that combines effective navigation with the balanced use of graphics, text and colour. Basic HTML and CSS skills will be applied to build web pages and sites with consideration given to the effects of computing platform on design choices.

Android Apps Development

Android App Development will open many doors for you since Android is the most popular operating system in the World. Build a strong foundation in Android Development, Android Studio, Virtual Machines and object-oriented Java Programming with this Complete, Up-to-date course. This is the Android Development Bootcamp you'll ever need to learn Android Development from scratch!
