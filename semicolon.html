SemiColon Tech Club.

Vision

We encourage our students to explore the joy of technology on their own instead of being limited by the syllabus.

Mission

The club organizes the annual technical fest Colossuem for the department, to help in encouraging curiosity and interest toward the domain amongst the students. The Information Technology Department of RCOEM organises a variety of events throughout the academic year. The events balance both technical and non-technical activities. The events consist of branch installation, cultural night, technical games, quizzes and a lot more.

Alacrity

The department organizes a cultural event filled with fun and frolic to welcome new students to the department.It is filled with creative competitions and is organised with a lot of enthusiasm and energy by the students. Various exhibitions of dance and music are prepared and presented by the students which sets the tone for an evening filled with laughter and entertainment.

Colosseum

The Department organises a 3 day long event aimed at providing co-curricular exposure to its students. The students participates in various fun activites and compete with students of other colleges as well. Students also prepare a departmental magazine featuring technical work, write-ups and artwork of their own.

National Level Technical Event “Colosseum19”

Information Technology department conducted the mega event “COLOSSEUM-19” – A national level technical symposium, under Semicolon Tech Club (STC) and CSI Students Chapter of RCOEM during 15-16 February, 2019. Mr. Sarang Bondre, Director (Business and Financial Services), Cognizant. Pune was the Chief Gust for the inaugural function The event spanning two days consists of different competitions like GROUND ZERO, EXPRESSO (Public Speaking), CODEMANIA, PAPER PRESENTATION, BOX CRICKET. etc. Prof. P. J. Assudani conducted two days’ workshop on “Cloud Computing and AWS Platform” during 14-15 February 2019. The event saw overwhelming participations from many colleges across the region.
