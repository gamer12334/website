Student Society of Information Technology

AIM

Aim of SSIT is to create opportunities for leadership development, learning, student engagement, and fostering of shared interests.

OBJECTIVE

This society is responsible for conducting various technical and co-curricular activities in the department. Departments' events like Alacrity, Colosseum and ITPL are organised and managed by SSIT.

Description

The society focuses on inculcating the moral values, confidence and an outgoing spirit in its members and the students. It tries to establish a community where students can learn and grow.
